Inset Cards

CSS classes: .inset-card, .inset-card--{sm|md|lg}

Sizes

Small (sm) — compact padding for tight layouts
Medium (md) — balanced padding for most contexts
Large (lg, default) — generous padding for standalone sections

Nested in Content Card

Summary
Inset cards are commonly used inside content cards to highlight secondary information.

With Rich Content

Note

Inset cards can contain any content — text, icons, lists, or other components. The subtle background and border distinguish them from the surrounding content.
