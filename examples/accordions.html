What is G2?

G2 is the world's largest and most trusted software marketplace. More than 90 million people annually use G2 to make smarter software decisions.

How are reviews verified?

G2 uses a multi-step verification process including LinkedIn authentication, email verification, and human moderation to ensure review authenticity.

What are G2 Reports?

G2 Reports are quarterly rankings of software products based on user reviews and online presence. They include Grid, Index, and Momentum reports.

Buyer Intent data shows which companies are actively researching your product category on G2, helping you prioritize outreach to in-market buyers.

Understanding intent signals

Intent signals include category page views, competitor comparisons, alternative searches, and product profile visits. Higher signal counts indicate stronger purchase intent.

Integrating with your CRM

G2 Buyer Intent integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other CRMs to automatically surface intent data alongside your existing leads and accounts.
